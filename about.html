Sofia is currently pursuing her BFA in Digital Media Arts at San Jose State University. She's in constant pursuit to improve her creative practice, inspired from a young age by the art of sotrytelling, she moves through her practice as she does with life, "with an open heart". From her early years in preschool when she would assume a leadership role and with her classmates create a structure like that of 'The Emerald City' with recycled trash to in present day collaborating with friends and peers to create passion projects as she pursues her degree.

As of today, Sofia works with softwares like the Adobe Creative Cloud, including Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, Light Room, Premiere Pro; she illustrates and designs digitally with a number of these softwares as well as Procreate. She has a soft background in 3D design softwares like Maya and Blender

Outside of her technical practice, she is incredibly passionate about a number of genres in film, though, high fantasy remains her favorite. As of today some of the films that have moved her deeply include, About Time, the Lord of the Rings saga, Perfect Days, and Everything Everywhere all at Once, with honorable mentions to Ghibli films.
