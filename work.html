Tag, Your're It!

Based on previous character render, a short story animation was made in Maya with a rendered environment and side character. The story follows our faun main character in conflict with a butterfly that wants to play tag with the faun.

Little Wooden Boy

This stop-motion short film is based on the film Pinocchio by Guillermo del Toro, a project self-produced by hand. The production of this project was over two weeks, devoted entirely to the crafting of the Pinocchio puppet and another extra week for animating the movement of the puppet in the self-crafted scenarios. Once the on-hand production was done, I took all the animated scenes and put them all together in Adobe After Effects, adding additional effects and sound to the final piece.

DIME

Based on both music produced by Silvana Estrada and personal experience, this music short film was produced, directed, and ( attempted ) acting, by me with the graceful collaboration of my friends for camera work and co-star. Video production was done in Adobe After Effects and filmed on my iPhone.
